Good To Know

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to provide water or power?
No. Every appointment arrives with an onboard water tank, generator and reclaim system, so we're fully self-contained — including at marinas and parking garages that don't allow outside hoses.
How long does an appointment take?
Express Refresh typically runs 45–60 minutes, Signature Detail 1.5–2.5 hours, and Showroom Correction or Ceramic Reserve are scheduled in advance with a longer on-site window, since correction work is done by hand.
Can you come to my office, marina or hotel valet?
Yes — anywhere in Miami-Dade County with legal parking access, including office garages, marina docks and hotel valet stands. Mention the location when you book so we can plan equipment access.
What happens if it rains?
Covered spots — garages, carports, hangars — proceed as scheduled. Fully open-air appointments are rescheduled at no charge if conditions won't hold a finish.
Do you detail exotics, classics and EVs?
Yes. We use pH-neutral, correction-safe products and soft pads suited to delicate clears, ceramic coatings and PPF, across everyday daily drivers, exotics, classics and EVs alike.
Is overnight service really available?
Genuinely, yes — we operate around the clock. A lot of clients book late at night so the car is ready for an early flight, a morning meeting, or just to wake up to a clean driveway.
